auto_awesome KEY CONCEPTS

Quantum mechanics is a fundamental theory in physics that provides a description of the physical properties of nature at the scale of atoms and subatomic particles. It is the foundation of all quantum physics including quantum chemistry, quantum field theory, quantum technology, and quantum information science.

Wave-Particle Duality: Every particle or quantum entity may be described as either a particle or a wave.

Uncertainty Principle: Formulated by Werner Heisenberg, stating that the position and velocity of an object cannot both be measured exactly, at the same time.

Superposition: A fundamental principle of quantum mechanics that holds that a physical system—such as an electron—exists partly in all its particular theoretically possible states simultaneously.
